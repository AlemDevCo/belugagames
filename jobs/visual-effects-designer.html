About Us
Hey! We are Beluga Games. A remote-first game development studio creating the next generation of new and unique experiences on the Roblox platform. Founded in early 2020, we have a team of over 10 developers working together on Battle Island, and one more new project. Our team is built of developers from all across the world!
About the job
Our team is looking for an experienced Visual Effects artist to create cartoony, unique, and awesome visuals effects for Battle Island! We are looking for effects like fire, explosion, smoke, ice, trails, and more. These effects will be used for extra detail on assets like vehicles and weapons. We are looking for a clean and cartoon look, with not too much detail.

  • Enthusiastic, respectful, active, and creative person
  • Flexible with pricing
