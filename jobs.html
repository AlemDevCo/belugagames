About Us
We're Awes0me Studios, an independent game development studio on Roblox. Founded in 2020, we are a diverse team of innovative developers. Our goal is to create new and unique experiences that users would love, all over the world, and available across all devices. Our team consists of talented and creative builders, modelers, programmers, designers, managers, animators and composers. We work together as a team to create awesome experiences for our players.
Why Work With Us
Diverse and Inclusive environment
Our team is built of developers from all across the globe. We welcome anyone to our diverse and inclusive team.
Create fun and unique games
Like creating fun and unique experiences for other users to enjoy? We do just that! Be apart of the fun and help us create the next generation of entertainment!
Friendly & Collaborative
We run a place where everyone is respectful and honest. Our developers work together as a team to get things done.
Creative and Involved workspace
Our team consists of many creative and talented developers, who are passionate for creating. All team members have an equal opportunity to lead, bring new ideas to the table, and have impact on our games.
Flexible Times
You choose when to work, and your breaks. Everyone has a different schedule. We give you a flexible schedule, to avoid stress, and burnout.
Fully Remote
We are a fully remote game studio, so you have the flexibility to work right from you house, or anywhere on the planet!
