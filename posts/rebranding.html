Why Did We Rebrand?
07 / 22 / 22

Hey everyone! We’ve just rebranded from “Awes0me Studios” to “Beluga” (Beluga Games) a lot of you have been wondering why, so here’s all the answers:

I’ve been planning this change for months, and I just got the name available today. I wanted to make this change for a few reasons. Most importantly, understanding of our brand/studio. It’s really hard for me and others to explain the name, website, studio, etc to people without having to make sure they spell it with a 0 instead of an O. Another reason is that I think it’s cleaner and simpler this way, both the name and logo. All of the group names, our socials, and website were updated accordingly. I hope you guys understand and accept the change. Our team is working on some awesome stuff, and massive updates for both of our games. Thank you!

- Murilo
