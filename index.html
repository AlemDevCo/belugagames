About Us

We're Awes0me Studios, an independent game development studio on Roblox. Founded in 2020, we are a diverse and innovative team of over 10 game developers. Our goal is to create new and unique experiences that users all around the world enjoy.

Our team loves creating games! And for the reason, we want all users to be able to access, understand, and play the game. Our games are compatible on Computer, Phone, Tablet, and XBOX.
