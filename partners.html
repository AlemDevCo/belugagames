What is it?
The Beluga Games Partner Program is a program made for content creators to partner up with us and make videos on our games. This is a way for us to expand our games and community. By making videos on us, you help us a lot, and you also get some cool perks.
How Does It Work?

We provide you with resources to create awesome content

You create videos promoting our games

We'll help you boost your video and channel

Earn awesome partner-exclusive perks

Who's Eligible?

    We are looking for fun & engaging content that anyone can enjoy. Here are some requirements, but we might make exceptions

  • YouTube channel with 750+ subs and/or 1k+ views a video
  • Twitch channel with 100+ followers and/or 20+ average viewers
  • Your channel should have a lot of Roblox content
  • Your channel must be kid-friendly, no inappropriate content or cursing
